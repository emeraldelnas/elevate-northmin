ELEVATE is a nationwide student movement which aims to take students to the next LEVEL. We want every student to experience a Life Empowered through Values, Excellence, and Leadership.

Mission

To honor God and make Christ-committed students who will make Christ-committed students.

Vision

Our vision is to see a movement of millions of committed students of the Lord Jesus Christ meeting in small groups and transforming lives, families, campuses, communities, nations, for the glory of God.
